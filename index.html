Bhala fek divas par Surguja ke Utkarsh or Shriyank ka dabdaba

7 August ko hui tritya rajyastariya bhala fek pratyogita mei under-16 mei Utkarsh ne pratham(40.91m) or Shriyank nei tritya sthan prapt kiya. Yah pratyogita Bilaspur ke Bahatrai Stadium mei hui.yah dono hi chatra Shiv Kumar ji(former Asian Games medalist and current NIS athletic coach) ke paas training lete hain. Ve Gandhi Stadium(Ambikapur) mei training dete hain.

Training Problems

Lack of resources

We don't have proper resources so as to reach to our Dream and goal. We are not provided with a proper equipments, so that we could do better training. We need some equipments like hurdles, high jump accessories, etc

Lack of financial support

Along with lack of resources, we don't have financial support, too. Not all the athletes have sufficient finance, so as to get proper diet, athletic-kit(shoes, spikes and all), etc.
